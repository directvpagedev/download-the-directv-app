What Is the DIRECTV App?

The DIRECTV app allows you to access your DIRECTV subscription from mobile and streaming devices. With the app, you can stream live channels, browse on-demand content, schedule recordings, and even manage your DVR remotely. It supports DIRECTV STREAM and satellite customers, depending on your subscription.

Supported Devices for DIRECTV App

  • Android smartphones and tablets
  • iPhone and iPad (iOS)
  • Amazon Fire TV and Firestick
  • Roku streaming devices
  • Apple TV
  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, etc.)
  • Web browsers (via directv.com)

How to Download DIRECTV App on Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your device.
  2. Search for “DIRECTV” or “DIRECTV Stream.”
  3. Select the official DIRECTV app from AT&T/DIRECTV.
  4. Tap Install and wait for it to download.
  5. Once installed, open the app and sign in with your DIRECTV credentials.

How to Download DIRECTV App on iPhone/iPad

  1. Open the App Store on your device.
  2. Search for “DIRECTV” or “DIRECTV Stream.”
  3. Tap Get to install the app.
  4. Once downloaded, open the app and log in with your account details.

How to Install DIRECTV App on Firestick or Fire TV

  1. Navigate to the Amazon Appstore on your Firestick.
  2. Search for “DIRECTV.”
  3. Select the app and click Download.
  4. Once installed, launch it and sign in.

Can You Use DIRECTV on Roku?

Yes, the DIRECTV STREAM app is available for Roku. Simply search for it in the Roku Channel Store, add the channel, and log in using your DIRECTV account.

How to Use the App After Installation

After installing, open the app and sign in using your DIRECTV user ID and password. You’ll be able to:

  • Stream live TV channels
  • Browse and watch on-demand shows and movies
  • Access your DVR recordings
  • Set parental controls and profile settings

Troubleshooting Installation Issues

  • Make sure your device has a stable internet connection
  • Ensure your operating system is up to date
  • Restart your device if the app won’t open or crashes
  • If unavailable in your app store, check if the app supports your device model
